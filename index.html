Andrew Atkinson

Artist · Educator · Curriculum Developer

I am an artist, educator, and curriculum developer. My work connects art, technology, and teaching: I have designed curriculum for Higher education, K-12 classrooms and teacher professional development, developed and taught undergraduate and graduate art and design courses, including creative coding courses, and produced full professional redesigns of foundation curriculum texts.

This portfolio documents five bodies of work spanning K-12 JavaScript curriculum and teacher professional development at Vidcode (2017-2019), undergraduate creative coding courses at Montclair State University (2019-2025), and a book design and production project for the Institute for the Advancement of Philosophy for Children (2021-present).
