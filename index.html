Andrew Atkinson

Artist · Educator · Curriculum Developer

I'm an artist and Assistant Professor of Digital Photography at Montclair State University, where I've taught visual arts, digital media, and creative coding since 2004. This portfolio documents a focused body of curriculum development work I contributed to at Vidcode between 2017 and 2019.

Vidcode was an edtech platform that taught middle and high school students to code in JavaScript through creative projects: video filters, animations, interactive art. It was built to support teachers with no coding background and to slot into any subject area, from art class to history. I joined the team after completing a software development program at Fullstack Academy, bringing a perspective shaped by years of teaching artists to think with digital tools.
